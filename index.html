Top Destinations & Highlights Destinasi & Tarikan Utama

⛰️ The Great Wall ⛰️ Tembok Besar China

Trek along the legendary, winding mountain ridges of the Mutianyu section with stunning panoramic views. Mendaki permatang gunung legenda yang bengkak-bengkok di bahagian Mutianyu dengan pemandangan panaroma yang menakjubkan.

🏯 The Forbidden City 🏯 Kota Larangan

Walk through the majestic gates of the massive Imperial Palace and stand in awe of historic dynasties. Berjalan melalui pintu gerbang megah di Istana Empayar yang luas dan hayati sejarah dinasti purba.

🚲 Hutong & Peking Duck 🍖 🚲 Hutong & Itik Peking 🍖

Take a traditional Rickshaw ride through historic Hutong Alleys followed by an authentic Peking Duck Banquet. Naiki beca tradisional melalui Lorong Lama Hutong diikuti dengan jamuan makan malam Itik Peking yang asli.

🌳 The Summer Palace 🌳 Istana Musim Panas

Relax amidst the breathtaking landscapes, peaceful royal pavilions, and serene lakes of the ancient Emperors. Bertenang di tengah-tengah landskap yang indah, paviliun diraja, dan tasik yang tenang milik Maharaja purba.

🏟️ Olympic Landmarks 🏟️ Mercu Tanda Olimpik

Capture spectacular photographs at iconic architectural masterpieces like the Bird's Nest and the Water Cube. Ambil foto yang menakjubkan di karya seni bina ikonik seperti Stadium Sarang Burung dan Kiub Air.

🎡 Universal Studios 🎡 Universal Studios Beijing

Experience an action-packed, thrilling day of blockbuster entertainment and world-class movie magic rides. Alami hari yang penuh keriangan dengan hiburan blockbuster hebat dan tarikan taman tema bertaraf dunia.

Ultimate 5D4N Itinerary Plan Pelan Itinerary 5H4M Utama

DAY 1 HARI 1

Arrival & Imperial Legends Ketibaan & Legenda Empayar

DAY 2 HARI 2

DAY 3 HARI 3

Movie Magic & Thrills Keajaiban Filem & Keriangan

Spend a full, action-packed day inside Universal Studios Beijing exploring cinematic worlds and rides. Satu hari penuh di Universal Studios Beijing menerokai dunia perfileman dan pelbagai permainan menakjubkan.

DAY 4 HARI 4

Royal Retreats & Cultural Alleys Keindahan Diraja & Lorong Budaya

Stroll across the serene lakes of The Summer Palace. Later, climb onto a traditional Rickshaw for a culture-filled ride through local Hutong Alleyway Tours. Bersiar-siar di sekitar tasik yang tenang di Istana Musim Panas (The Summer Palace). Kemudian, naiki beca tradisional untuk menerokai warisan budaya di sekitar lorong kuno Hutong.

DAY 5 HARI 5

Modern Landmarks & Farewell Ikon Moden & Selamat Tinggal
